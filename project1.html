Chores Mobile Application

The goal of this project is to create a mobile app that will help parents encourage their children to do chores using a reward-based method. The app enables parents to create goals, offer rewards, and give children with a visually appealing interface. The design is user-centric, taking into account the demands of the user.

Overview

In-depth information about the characteristics and interests of the user—Laura, a 38-year-old accountant and mother of two—will be included in this project on the creating of user persona. It will also feature several high-fidelity prototypes that show how users will interact with the app for two important tasks. The design choices are supported by well-established design theories and principles, guaranteeing that the chore management app was developed with a comprehensive and user-centric approach.

Design Process

The step by step process for creating this application.

  • Research: Using user persona.
  • Wireframing: Early sketches of layouts.
  • Prototyping: High fidelity prototypes in Figma.
  • Task flow: 1. Adding tasks and rewards / 2. Monitor and progress.
  • Testing: Usability test - Think aloud.
